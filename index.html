Why Us

We believe that one day present vaccine preventable diseases such as typhoid, malaria, hepatitis B, cervical cancer, rota virus, polio just to mention a few will be extinct like small pox, and that we plan achieving by screening and vaccinating populations to create more herd communities. This we do through Inocul8 a mobile on-demand vaccination clinic service platform.

What We Do

Inocul8 is a mobile on-demand vaccination clinic service platform that takes vaccination and screening services to clients at their comfort, offering a prompt, flexible, and convenient service while ensuring our clients are healthy, protected, and safe from vaccine preventable diseases..

Flexible

We believe people can get vaccinated no matter how busy they are, that is why our team go the extra mile to ensure you and your family get vaccinated in a flexible fashion.

Convinenece

We come over to you at the comfort of your homes or offices to administer your vaccines thus saving you time that would have been spent waiting in the hospital for your shots..

Affordable

We believe cost shouldn’t hinder people from getting vaccinated that’s why we organize highly subsidised screening and vaccination outreaches for people in ultra poor communities through CBPI Foundation.

Quality

Our service are provided by young licensed and qualified medical professionals in Nigeria.

Safety

All safety precautions are followed and imbibed when rendering our services
